Towers of Hanoi Problem Using Prolog
Prolog Program (Towers of Hanoi)
hanoi(0, _, _, _) :- !.
hanoi(N, Source, Target, Auxiliary) :-
N > 0,
M is N - 1,
hanoi(M, Source, Auxiliary,
Target), write('Move disk '),
write(N), write(' from '),
write(Source), write(' to '),
write(Target), nl, hanoi(M,
Auxiliary, Target, Source).

Sample Query
?- hanoi(3, left, right, middle).

Expected Output
Move disk 1 from left to right
Move disk 2 from left to middle
Move disk 1 from right to middle
Move disk 3 from left to right Move
disk 1 from middle to left
Move disk 2 from middle to right
Move disk 1 from left to right
